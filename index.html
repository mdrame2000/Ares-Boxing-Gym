About Us

Ares is the Greek God of War and courage amongs other things. Here at Ares Boxing Gym we model our gym after these values of war and courage as they are fundamental in the sport of boxing. We offer classes in boxing for all age groups starting from 6 years old at our sole location in Manhattan, New York.

For those unable to come to the gym we are now bringing workouts to you thanks to our diversely gifted group of trainers.
